# Mind and Peace Connection Center – Website

This repository contains a static, accessible, mobile-friendly website concept for **Mind and Peace Connection Center**, a holistic, evidence-based mental health clinic with a focus on:

- Anxiety & depression care  
- Trauma & PTSD care  
- Adolescent & family support  

The site is designed with:

- Formal, clinical-but-kind language  
- Trauma-informed and non-stigmatizing content  
- Mobile-first, responsive layouts  
- Accessibility-conscious structure (semantic HTML, clear headings, strong contrast)  

---

## Files

- `index.html`  
  Homepage with:
  - Hero section
  - “I’m looking for help with…” triage cards
  - Integrative approach overview
  - Services overview
  - Team preview
  - Teletherapy section
  - Resources preview
  - Contact form & crisis disclaimer

- `anxiety-depression.html`  
  Service page for anxiety & depression care with:
  - Service-specific hero
  - “Understanding anxiety and depression”
  - “How we can help”
  - “What to expect”
  - “Is this service right for you?”
  - FAQ
  - Call-to-action & crisis note

- `trauma-ptsd.html`  
  Service page for trauma & PTSD care with:
  - Service-specific hero
  - “Understanding trauma and post-traumatic stress”
  - Trauma-informed approach
  - “What to expect”
  - “Is this service right for you?”
  - FAQ
  - Call-to-action & crisis note

- `adolescent-family.html`  
  Service page for adolescent & family support with:
  - Service-specific hero
  - “Understanding adolescent mental health”
  - “How we work with teenagers”
  - “How we involve families”
  - “What to expect”
  - “Is this service right for you?”
  - FAQ
  - Call-to-action & crisis note

- `styles.css`  
  Global stylesheet containing:
  - Color system (calm blues, sage, warm sand, charcoal text)
  - Typography, spacing, and layout utilities
  - Reusable components:
    - Header, navigation, footer
    - Hero card “therapy room” illustration
    - Card grids
    - Teletherapy layout
    - Contact form styling
    - Shared **two-column content layout** (`.section-two-column`, `.two-column`, `.two-column-aside`)
