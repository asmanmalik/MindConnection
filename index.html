Care in person or online

We provide both in-person and secure online appointments when appropriate. Teletherapy can expand access to care while maintaining the same standard of professionalism and respect.

Teletherapy basics

  • Secure video sessions with the same clinicians you would see in person.
  • Flexible scheduling options when feasible.
  • Guidance on how to prepare your space for privacy and minimal distraction.

How it works

  • Discuss whether teletherapy is appropriate during your consultation.
  • Receive instructions for accessing the secure platform and completing forms.
  • Log in to your session at the scheduled time.
